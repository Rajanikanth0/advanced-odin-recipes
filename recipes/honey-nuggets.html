Honey Nuggets

Description

Honey Nuggets is a Meat Food item, Made up of Honey. It serves as fast food, When consumed, it restores 37.5 hunger, 20 Health and 5 Sanity.

Ingredients

  • Honey
  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Berries

Steps

  1. Prepare a Crock Pot
  2. Add Delicious Honey
  3. Paste the sliced meat sticks
  4. Sprinkle Chopped Vegetables
  5. Add juice of berries
  6. Let it cook for 40 seconds
