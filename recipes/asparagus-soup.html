Asparagus Soup

Description

Asparagus Soup, as it's name defines it's a soup Food. It's main item is Asparagus. Made up of vegetables food items. When consumed, it restores 18.75 hunger, 20 Health and 5 Sanity.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • Tomato
  • Potato
  • Carrot

Steps

  1. Add Chopped tomato
  2. Mix the Boiled Potato
  3. Then the Asparagus
  4. Sprinkle the Chopped Carrot
  5. Let it cook for 10 seconds
