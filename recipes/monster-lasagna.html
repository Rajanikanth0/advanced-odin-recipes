Monster Lasagna

Description

Monster Lasagna is a Meat Food, Made up of Monster food items. Can be used to feed animals, When consumed, it restores 37.5 hunger, but at a cost of 20 health and 20 sanity.

Ingredients

  • Monster meat
  • Monster jerky
  • Durians
  • Jellyfish

Steps

  1. Prepare a Crock Pot
  2. Add at least 2 Monster Food Items
  3. Fill the Crock Pot with any vegetables
  4. Close the Crock Pot.
  5. Let it cook for 10 seconds
